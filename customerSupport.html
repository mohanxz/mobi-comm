SIM Activation & Setup

  • How do I activate my new SIM card?
    Insert the new SIM into your phone, restart the device, and dial the activation number provided by your operator. You may receive an SMS confirmation.
  • How long does it take to activate a new SIM?
    Most SIM cards are activated within 1-4 hours. In some cases, it may take up to 24 hours.
  • Why is my new SIM not working?
    Ensure the SIM is inserted correctly, restart your phone, and check for network signals. If the issue persists, contact your operator.
  • Do I need to complete KYC to activate my SIM?
    Yes, some operators require identity verification (Aadhaar, passport, or driving license) before activating your SIM.
